RENT AND RENTAL MANAGEMENT

ABOUT

Income Tracker is an idea that arose from watching my father struggle to track rent collected from his tenants. A few of his tenants could live rent-free because of poor record keeping. While there are spreadsheets designed to help with income tracking, I believe a customizable app with visual features may be easier for him.

Initially, the project will be restricted to tracking rent but in the future, I will include other functionalities that allow tracking of client payments for other firms such as internet providers.

FEATURES

PROPERTY LISTING

Allows users to add, edit, and remove property listings with details such as property name, location, total rent, and occupancy rate.

TENANT MANAGEMENT

Keeps track of tenant information, including contact details, and payment histories.

FINANCIAL REPORT AND ANALYSIS

Provides analytics and insights into property performance, vacancy rates, and rental income trends.

CUSTOMIZATION

The system can be modified to ensure a landlord gets exactly what they need to manage their property.
